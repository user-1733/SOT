School Clubs

  • Art and Design Club

    The Art Club nurtures creativity through painting, drawing, sculpture, and digital media

  • IT Club

    Students work on real-world projects, participate in hackathons, and learn cutting-edge skills to prepare for the digital future.

  • Music Club

    Open to musicians and music lovers, this club offers opportunities to perform, compose, and discuss music

  • Anti drug and Addiction Club

    This club raises awareness about the dangers of substance abuse through workshops, guest speakers, and peer-led campaigns

  • Enviromental Club

    This club focuses on sustainability, organizing recycling programs, clean-up events, and awareness campaigns

  • Debate Club

    The Debate Club helps students develop critical thinking and public speaking skills through structured arguments on various topics.

  • Chemistry Club

    This club brings chemistry to life with explosive experiments, lab work, and problem-solving challenges.

  • Physics Club

    From building hovercrafts to studying astrophysics, the Physics Club tackles hands-on experiments and theoretical puzzles.

  • Space Science Club

    This club explores astronomy, rocketry, and space technology through telescope observations, model launches, and NASA challenges.

  • MUN Club

    MUN simulates United Nations debates, where students represent countries, negotiate solutions, and discuss global issues

  • Doctors Club

    The club prepares students for healthcare careers with hands-on practice and expert guest speakers.

  • Book Club

    Members recommend reads, analyze themes, and even meet authors, deepening their love for literature.

  • Helpers Club

    Focused on community service, this club organizes charity drives, tutoring programs, and neighborhood clean-ups

    • Chess Club
